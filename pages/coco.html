Hello, my name is Coco! I have been a licensed manicurist since 2011. A lot has changed in the beauty industry since then and I strive to make sure I keep up with those changes. I have always been passionate about art and that's what attracted me to the industry. I enjoy encapsulated nail art and hand painted abstract designs of acrylic and gel polish nails. I decided to open Embellished Nails and Spa in 2021 to provide a safe and welcoming environment, not only for our clients, but also for the service providers who work here.
